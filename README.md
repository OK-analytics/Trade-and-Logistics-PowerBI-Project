**Sales & Revenue Performance (The "How much?" perspective)**

* Gross Merchandise Value (GMV): Monitored the total value of merchandise sold to track overall business scale.

* Average Order Value (AOV): Calculated the average spend per transaction to understand customer purchasing power.

* Revenue vs. LY (Last Year): \[Custom Metric] Implemented Year-over-Year analysis to identify growth trends and seasonal peaks.

* Lead-to-Deal Conversion: Evaluated the efficiency of the sales funnel by tracking potential leads versus closed transactions.

**Operational \& Logistics Efficiency (The "How fast?" perspective)**

* Order Fulfillment Rate: Analyzed the funnel from "Approved" to "Shipped" and "Delivered" (achieving a 97.66% successful delivery rate).

* Delivery Lead Time: Measured the average time from order placement to customer receipt to optimize carrier performance.

* Shipping Bottleneck Identification: Visualized stages where orders stall to improve supply chain velocity.

**Payment Dynamics \& Cash Flow (The "How paid?" perspective)**

* Payment Mix Analysis: Identified that Credit Cards drive nearly 74% of total revenue, followed by Boleto and Vouchers.

* Average Installment Rate: \[Custom Metric] Tracked the average number of installments (2.85) to assess credit-driven buying behavior.

* Voucher Impact Tracking: Measured the volume of transactions using vouchers to evaluate the effectiveness of loyalty programs.

**Customer Experience \& Quality Control (The "How happy?" perspective)**

* Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT): Visualized the Average Review Score (4.09/5) to monitor overall brand health.

* Response Time Analysis: Measured the correlation between response time and customer ratings to improve service levels.
