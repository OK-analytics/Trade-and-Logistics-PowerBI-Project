\*\* Sales \& Revenue Performance (The "How much?" perspective)\*\*

&nbsp;\* Gross Merchandise Value (GMV): Monitored the total value of merchandise sold to track overall business scale.

&nbsp;\* Average Order Value (AOV): Calculated the average spend per transaction to understand customer purchasing power.

&nbsp;\* Revenue vs. LY (Last Year): \[Custom Metric] Implemented Year-over-Year analysis to identify growth trends and seasonal peaks.

&nbsp;\* Lead-to-Deal Conversion: Evaluated the efficiency of the sales funnel by tracking potential leads versus closed transactions.

\*\* Operational \& Logistics Efficiency (The "How fast?" perspective)\*\*

&nbsp;\* Order Fulfillment Rate: Analyzed the funnel from "Approved" to "Shipped" and "Delivered" (achieving a 97.66% successful delivery rate).

&nbsp;\* Delivery Lead Time: Measured the average time from order placement to customer receipt to optimize carrier performance.

&nbsp;\* Shipping Bottleneck Identification: Visualized stages where orders stall to improve supply chain velocity.

\*\* Payment Dynamics \& Cash Flow (The "How paid?" perspective)\*\*

&nbsp;\* Payment Mix Analysis: Identified that Credit Cards drive nearly 74% of total revenue, followed by Boleto and Vouchers.

&nbsp;\* Average Installment Rate: \[Custom Metric] Tracked the average number of installments (2.85) to assess credit-driven buying behavior.

&nbsp;\* Voucher Impact Tracking: Measured the volume of transactions using vouchers to evaluate the effectiveness of loyalty programs.

\*\* Customer Experience \& Quality Control (The "How happy?" perspective)\*\*

&nbsp;\* Response Time Analysis: Measured the correlation between response time and customer ratings to improve service levels.
